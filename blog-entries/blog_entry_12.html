Lecture

In this lecture we just recapped Assignment 3 which was helpful as last time we went over it I was really stressed about making my CMS website and didn’t take a lot of it in. I am coding my blog! Hopefully, it goes well. Also discovered that I need to go back and add some more reflective thinking to my previous blog posts.

Assignment

This week I began working on coding my blog. I started by coding the base for the blog website. I chose to use flexboxes to structure my webpage, as they are very responsive but also because I am the most familiar with them. I put borders around all of the different sections so I could sort out bugs as they came up, which they very much did.
The one that took me the longest to work out was the navigation options in the nav bar. Initially, I had them as a list, but that wasn’t working, so I made them not a list and fiddled with the positioning and that worked. I have so far made the landing page and one blog entry page. I just chose to use my logo from my other website as a placeholder logo for now.

I then began going back over my blog entries from this trimester and got very annoyed at my past self (why did I only write a single sentence some weeks?). I added some more details to the ones that needed it and gathered images for each week. Eventually, I got to Week 12, which is what I am typing now (blog-ception). I still have a lot more work to do on my blog website, but I will have to do that next week.

Conclusion

This class taught me some really useful skills, and I’m not just saying that. I liked how classes were still collaborative even though they were online, and even though it was stressful at the time I like that we were forced to step outside our comfort zones to learn new techniques.
Overall, if I choose to make a website in future, I feel like I have all of the knowledge and resources I would need to do that, and for that I have this class to thank.
