Discussing accessibility was also helpful as my user persona has dyslexia, so I will need to focus on dyslexia-friendly fonts and high contrast between text and background.

Assignment

I was still very busy with other classes this week but did have some fun designing a colour palette for my website. I challenged myself to keep it simple (I have a habit of making colour palettes with about 10 different colours) and ended up choosing just classic black and white, some calming muted blue and purple shades (for reasons discussed above), and a more saturated blue for a pop of colour.
