Week 8 Blog: Website Design Cont.

Assignment

I started the week by designing the iteration of my second journey for my website Amiva. It was just as fiddly as the first one but was slightly easier/faster to produce as I already had a design style established. Speaking of style I finished off my style guide, containing my fonts, components, icons and more.
After designing all of the iterations, I worked on my high-fidelity prototype. I linked together all my pages and improved a few final things. I then wrote my design rationale, where I discussed in depth every design decision that went into making my website prototype. I tried to be as detailed as possible and I am confident that I covered everything that needed discussing.
I was then able to hand in my completed project, which was a huge relief. Honestly, this project was really difficult for me, but I learnt lots of really helpful skills that will definitely aid me in future, and for that I am thankful.
